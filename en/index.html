Our house

G. de Provence was born from a deep attachment to the region, its plants, its artisans and its art de vivre.

We offer skincare and home fragrances inspired by the Provençal landscape, combining natural ingredients, local expertise and a contemporary aesthetic.

We believe in a quiet luxury, in simple and true beauty shaped by light and wind, in products that tell a story: that of Provence, as it truly is.

G. de Provence – Natural luxury

From lavender to donkey milk, every ingredient is chosen for its sincerity, local roots and its ability to convey the gentleness of this land.

Our commitments

Preserving the authenticity of Provence and reinterpreting it with high standards.

Mastered origins

Provençal ingredients are prioritised, including our “Lavande Hautement Bleue”, sourced from selected local partners.

Clean formulas

Natural, certified organic and transparent compositions, conceived for the skin and for the air we breathe.

Respect for the land

100% local production, controlled circuits, savoir‑faire of distillers, master soap makers and artisans of Provence.

Eco‑responsibility

Recycled and noble packaging, sustainable raw materials, a global approach designed to last.

Sensory excellence

Smooth textures and authentic fragrances, crafted by “noses” who are heirs to the Grasse tradition.

A land, a light

At the heart of Provence, a particular light glides over white stones, lingers in lavender furrows and leaves an almost tangible softness in the air.

This landscape is not just a backdrop: it is a living heritage shaped by distillers, master soap makers, harvesters and artisans for generations.

Lavender

Harvested at dawn to preserve its freshness.

Donkey milk

From small farms that respect the animals’ natural rhythm.

Almond & olive
